NY.GOV - Terms of Service

You can use Online Services to:

  • File a claim for Unemployment Insurance Benefits
  • Claim weekly Unemployment Insurance Benefits
  • Change your method of payment
  • Change your tax withholding information
  • Check on the status of your payments
  • View/print your 1099G
  • Receive important messages from the Department of Labor
When you can file a claim online and access other online services
  • Monday to Thursday 7:30 am to 7:30 pm
  • Friday from 7:30 am to 5 pm
  • Weekends from 12:01 am Saturday to 7 pm Sunday
When you can claim weekly benefits
  • Sunday from 12:01 am to midnight
  • Monday to Friday from 7:30 am to midnight
  • Saturday from 12:01 am to midnight

If you already have a NY.GOV ID, please enter your Username and Password in the box on the left and click on 'Sign In'.

If you do not have a NY.GOV ID, click on 'Create Account'. You will be asked for some basic personal information, including an e-mail address you use regularly.

  • What is NY.GOV?

    NY.GOV ID is a system created by the NYS Office for Technology (OFT). It is used to create and store Usernames and Passwords. These allow you to access online services from various NYS government agencies. For example, if you create a NY.GOV ID through the Department of Labor and then want to use the Online Tax Center of the Department of Taxation and Finance, you can use the same NY.GOV ID Username and Password for both agencies.

    If you already have a NY.GOV ID Username and Password before you sign up with the Department of Labor, we will assign that Username and Password to your Department of Labor account.

  • Username Guidelines

    Your Username is the name you create when you first sign up with the Department of Labor. It becomes your NY.GOV ID Username. You use it to sign in to Online Services at the Department of Labor and other NYS government agencies. Your Username must contain letters and/or numbers or be an e-mail address.

  • Password Guidelines

    Your Password is a combination of characters that you create, along with your Username, to use our Online Services. It also becomes your NY.GOV ID Password. Your NY.GOV Username and Password can also be used for online services at other NYS government agencies.

    There are specific rules for the Password you create. Your Password must:

    • Be different from your Username
    • Contain at least 8 characters
    • Include at least 1 uppercase letter, 1 lowercase letter, and 1 number
    • Be different from your previous 12 Passwords

    Your Password should be something you can remember without writing it down. It should not be easy for other people to guess.

    When you create your Username and Password, you will be asked to answer personal security questions. If you should forget your Password, you will use these answers to prove your identity and reset your Password.

This includes entering your Username and Password. You will be held accountable for the actions of the person who assists you. You may be subject to penalties if you are not present when he/she enters our system on your behalf. Penalties may include forfeiture of benefits.
